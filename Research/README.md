 **Project Overview**

The Local Clinic Website is a modern and user-friendly healthcare website designed to help patients easily book appointments, explore medical services, and connect with certified doctors.

This project focuses on improving patient experience through clean layout, clear call-to-actions, and trust-building design elements.

**Problem Statement**

Many local clinics face issues such as:

Manual appointment booking through phone calls

Poor online presence

Lack of clear service information

Limited trust-building content

Patients often experience confusion while searching for reliable healthcare services.

**Solution**

This website provides:

Easy online appointment booking

Clear service listing

Doctor profiles with specialization

Emergency contact visibility

Trust indicators (experience, certified doctors, patient count)

The goal is to create a seamless and stress-free experience for patients.

**Target Users**

Patients looking for nearby healthcare services

Families booking appointments for children or elders

Emergency care seekers

Users preferring online booking over phone calls

**Tools Used**

Figma (UI Design & Prototyping)

Design principles (Hierarchy, Contrast, Spacing)

Responsive layout approach

**Screens Included**

Home Page

Services Page

About Us Page

Book Appointment Page

Doctor Profile Page

Mobile Responsive Screens

**Key Features**

Clean and minimal layout

Strong call-to-action buttons

Trust-building statistics section

Doctor cards with specializations

Working hours & emergency contact section

Responsive-friendly design structure
